# Product Overview

**Flowee** is an artisan flower shop landing page targeting Indonesian customers. The business offers fresh flower arrangements for various occasions including graduations, birthdays, anniversaries, condolences, and special events.

## Core Value Proposition

- **Fresh, artisan flower arrangements** designed for special moments
- **Fast response time** (< 5 minutes)
- **Custom designs** tailored to customer budget and preferences
- **Same-day delivery** available for orders before 2 PM

## Target Audience

Indonesian customers seeking premium flower arrangements for:
- Graduation ceremonies (buket wisuda)
- Romantic occasions (hand bouquets)
- Congratulatory events (papan bunga)
- Birthdays and celebrations
- Condolences and sympathy

## Business Model

- **Order channel**: WhatsApp-based ordering system
- **Pricing**: Starting from Rp 250,000 to Rp 500,000+ depending on arrangement type
- **Delivery**: Free shipping within 5km radius, flat rate beyond
- **Payment**: Bank transfer (BCA, Mandiri), e-wallets (GoPay, OVO, ShopeePay), QRIS

## Brand Identity

- **Tagline**: "Setiap Bunga, Cerita yang Mengalir" (Every Flower, A Flowing Story)
- **Tone**: Elegant, warm, artisan-focused
- **Visual style**: Natural color palette with green (#173901), gold (#885202), and cream (#fdf9f3) tones
- **Typography**: Playfair Display (serif, elegant) + DM Sans (modern, clean)
